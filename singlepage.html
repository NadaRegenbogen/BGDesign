About Us

We are location managment team based in Belgrade, Serbia, a country with many undiscovered locations.
From many inspiring places of industrial heritage we aim to find a perfect location for your movie, music video, comercial or photo shoot.
The most popular locations are abandoned factories, neglected railway routes and off course our star locations - WW2 monuments!
How it works:
After you contact us with your idea concept we will reply and connect in order to get into more details with you.
We will provide photos and video material of places you choose.
We can also organize a free tour for you if you wish to visit Serbia and check out the locations in person.
Important notice:
Since our cultural monuments are booked many months in advance we advise you to take that into consideration.
